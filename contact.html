Absolutely. We have catered for events ranging from 10 people to 500+. Our largest event to date was a 600-person corporate conference. We offer bespoke packages for any event size, with dedicated staff, custom menus, and full logistics management.

We require a minimum of 48 hours notice for catering orders. For larger events (50+ people) or bespoke packages, we recommend booking at least 5 business days in advance to ensure we can source all premium ingredients and allocate the right team.

Yes — the majority of our menu is suitable for vegans, and we have extensive gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, and halal options. Every menu item is clearly labelled with dietary information. Our team is trained to prevent cross-contamination for customers with serious allergies.

We currently deliver catering within a 15-mile radius of Greenville city centre. For events outside this zone, please contact us as we may be able to accommodate on a case-by-case basis, particularly for larger orders. Delivery is free for orders over £150.
