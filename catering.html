Quick Checklist

Catering Event Checklist

Simple guidelines to calculate quantities and plan your event menu layouts seamlessly.

1. Guest Count

Allow 1.5 servings per guest for
events under 3 hours, or 2.5 servings
for half-day workshops.

2. Dietary Diversity

Offer at least one vegan, one gluten-free, and one nut-free option. Label our juices clearly using our provided allergen table.

3. Ice & Chillers

Raw cold-pressed juices must stay below 4°C. Our setups include custom insulated catering ice trays at no extra cost.

4. Delivery Scheduling

Schedule delivery 45 minutes before the event starts to allow for table layout styling and cooling set-up.
