Pairing Science

How to Pair Your Combos

Maximize your body's absorption of nutrients by pairing our cold-pressed juices with the correct foods.

Vitamin C & Plant Iron

Maximize absorption of plant-based iron (found in avocado and toast) by pairing it with active Vitamin C juices (like orange or lemon based blends).

Fat-Soluble Nutrients

Beta-carotene and vitamins A/K in green juices require healthy fats to dissolve. Enjoy them alongside almond butter or chia bowls to absorb them fully.

Glycemic Balance

Juice contains natural fruit fructose. Drinking it alongside fiber-rich oats and protein bars slows digestion and prevents sudden blood sugar spikes.
