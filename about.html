How It Started

Our Story

FreshSip was born in 2016 from a simple frustration: founder Amelia Green could not find a place that served genuinely fresh, truly nutritious juices that tasted incredible without compromising on quality or ethics.

Starting with a single cold-press juicer in a small market stall, Amelia built a loyal community of health enthusiasts who shared her belief: what you put into your body matters, and it should also bring you joy.

Today, FreshSip serves over 800 customers daily from our flagship city-centre cafe, with a full menu of over 120 fresh items, catering services for corporate clients, and a growing community of loyal members.

What We Believe

Our Mission & Values

We are guided by a simple mission: to make genuinely healthy nutrition accessible, delicious, and joyful for everyone.

Nutrition First

Every recipe begins with nutritional science. We partner with registered nutritionists to ensure every product delivers genuine, measurable health benefits.

Sustainable Sourcing

We work exclusively with certified organic farms that use regenerative agriculture. Every supplier is personally vetted by our founder for ethical standards.

Community Focused

FreshSip gives back through our community nutrition programme — providing free wellness workshops and reduced-cost meals for local community groups monthly.

Innovation Driven

Our monthly R&D sessions explore cutting-edge nutritional science to continually elevate our menu with new superfoods, adaptogens, and functional ingredients.

Quality Guaranteed

Every batch of produce istested on arrival. We rejectanything that does not meetour stringent quality standards —regardless of cost or convenience.
